# Brand Style

## Brand Snapshot
Name: Sample Coffee Co.
Website: example only
Handles: @samplecoffee
Offer: Small-batch coffee for relaxed mornings.
Audience: Coffee drinkers who like useful, simple social content.
Primary goals: Awareness, saves, and community engagement.

## Voice
Tone: Warm, concise, useful.
Words to use: morning ritual, fresh roast, simple, calm.
Words to avoid: miracle, hustle, guaranteed, best ever.
Point of view: Good coffee can make a normal morning feel considered.
CTA style: Soft and direct.

## Visual Identity
Palette: Deep navy, warm cream, sea-glass green, espresso brown, soft white.
Typography: Clean editorial sans-serif.
Photography style: Natural light, ceramic mugs, warm counters, simple props.
Layout style: Clean, readable, story-led.
Do: Keep scenes calm and relevant.
Do not: Invent product packaging, awards, or unsupported claims.

## Content Pillars
1. Morning rituals
2. Coffee education
3. Behind the scenes
4. Community

## Compliance And Risk
Claims to avoid: Health, productivity, or performance claims.
Disclaimers: Confirm offers, prices, dates, and terms before publishing.
Approval requirements: Use real product photos for real packaging.
